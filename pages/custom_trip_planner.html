Planning Questions?

Common questions about creating your custom Uganda adventure

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for gorilla trekking permits which have limited availability. For peak season travel (June-September, December-February), booking 6-12 months ahead ensures the best availability and rates.

Yes, we understand plans can change. Modifications are possible up to 60 days before departure, subject to availability and any applicable fees. Some permits (like gorilla trekking) have strict cancellation policies, so we'll work with you to find the best solution.

Our quotes include accommodation, meals as specified, private 4x4 safari vehicle with driver-guide, park entrance fees, and activities listed in your itinerary. International flights, visas, travel insurance, tips, and personal expenses are typically not included but can be arranged.

Yes! Groups of 6 or more travelers receive special rates. We also offer customized group planning tools and can coordinate complex logistics for larger parties. Contact us for a personalized group quote.
