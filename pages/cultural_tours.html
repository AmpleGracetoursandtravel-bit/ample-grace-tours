Cultural Respect Guidelines

Essential guidelines for respectful cultural interactions and meaningful community connections

Photography Etiquette

  • Always ask permission before photographing people
  • Respect sacred spaces and ceremonies
  • Share photos with community members when possible
  • Follow your guide's instructions in sensitive areas

Cultural Interactions

  • Greet elders and community leaders first
  • Dress modestly, especially in rural areas
  • Accept hospitality graciously
  • Learn basic greetings in local languages

Meaningful Giving

  • Support community projects rather than individual handouts
  • Purchase crafts directly from artisans
  • Consult guides about appropriate gifts
  • Focus on sustainable, long-term impact
